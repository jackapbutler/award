Transformative effect all sectors.

AI has already had a transformative impact across fields such as linguistics through language understanding & translation, biology through protein folding and art through image generation.

Award Motivation

I think it's extremely important to encourage and motivate the next generation of mathematicians and computer scientists to tackle the hard societal and technical problems within the field of artificial intelligence.

Ultimately it will be the next generation of enthusiastic students that will be responsible for developing the current profound breakthroughs in AI and building on top of this knowledge in future endeavours. My central motivation for creating this award is to encourage the next generation of AI leaders to pursue further education in the field.

Jack Butler
